# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in contributing to this project!

Please note that Contributions you make become the property of Pregnancy Aid of Washington State.
In other words, you are donating your ideas and works, giving up any claim to copyright or
licensing restrictions for your Contributions. By "Contribution" we mean anything that is
intentionally submitted to us by any form of electronic, verbal, or written communication,
excluding communication that is conspicuously marked or otherwise designated in writing by
the copyright owner as "Not a Contribution."

Pregancy Aid of Washington State is a 501(c)(3) volunteer-based charity.
