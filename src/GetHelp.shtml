How do families get help from Pregnancy Aid?

If you're pregnant or have young children, and you need help, YOU ARE NOT ALONE. Pregnancy Aid cares about you and is ready to help you.

Help is as near as the Pregnancy Aid office in your community.

We have a presence up and down the Puget Sound area from Olympia to Everett. Simply come on in during our hours of operation. You are welcome to drop into our offices any time. No appointment is ever needed. Find an office near you.

We can help you get the information you need to make important decisions.

We can help provide you with material support, including infant clothing, car seats, maternity clothing, and more.

Check out our list of services.

Services are always provided without charge by friendly volunteers who care for you.
