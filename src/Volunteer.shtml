You can help too!

Your assistance is essential to the work of Pregnancy Aid

Pregnancy Aid depends upon private contributes of time, money, and in-kind donations. Our needs vary by location. Services provided by our volunteers include helping during office hours, supporting our computer and media systems, making sure car seats and cribs are safe, and repairing or cleaning baby items.

Money and in-kind donations

Please send your financial or in-kind donation to the nearest Pregnancy Aid office. Some offices can accept an online donation. Donations are generally tax-deductible. We will be happy to provide you with a receipt for your donation.

Even a couple of hours a week is appreciated!

If you can help as a volunteer, working in one of our offices, or even in your own home, please visit, call, or email your nearest office for a volunteer application. Some volunteers work with clients, some sew at home, others manage a social media account or help with fund-raising. Both professional and non-professional services are welcome.

Find an office near you and join our mission to help our communities!
