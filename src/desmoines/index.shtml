Shop free for babies, children, and mothers needing help

We welcome mothers and family members. They choose what they need from among clean new and gently used items donated to us, including:

  • maternity clothes and nursing supplies
  • baby diapers, clothes, and supplies
  • baby equipment such as portable cribs, car seats, and strollers
  • children's clothes up to size 6
  • toys and books

Shoppers who register with their ID may return to shop every 30 days. We keep their personal information confidential.

We are a nonprofit run entirely by volunteers

Drop by with items to donate or with a monetary donation and ask about volunteer opportunities.

Cash donations help us buy high-demand items such as diapers, as well as pay for supplies, rent, telephone service, and other expenses.

Volunteers work in our shop during operating hours sorting donations and helping shoppers. Off-hour and work-from-home volunteers help with laundry, administrative duties, and other tasks.

We are not religiously sponsored. We offer no medical or counseling services.
