Our Services

Pregnancy Aid helps by providing:

  • Decision making information
  • Referrals to prenatal care
  • Layettes for infants and those with special needs or those who are premature
  • Car seats and strollers
  • Positive emotional support
  • Maternity clothing
  • Baby and children's clothing
  • Infant formula and diapers
  • Baby furniture
  • Referrals for other services
  • Additional services as needed and available

Perhaps the most important service, though, is the friendship and concern that is given to every person who contacts Pregnancy Aid.
