Who We Are

What is Pregnancy Aid?

Pregnancy Aid is a non-sectarian social services agency that provides immediate, confidential help to any family with a pregnant mom or a child aged 5 years or younger.

Personal, practical and emotional support are given on a one-to-one basis at no charge and with no strings atached.

How can Pregnancy Aid provide these services without charge to its clients?

Pregnancy Aid is a non-profit 501(c)3, run by volunteers and funded by generous donors who truly care. Passionate volunteers contribute thousands of hours every year to ensure that Pregnancy Aid is available for those in need.
